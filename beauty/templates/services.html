The word "Softpllow" is translated from Kazakh as "softness". Our complex is a truly inexhaustible source of eternal youth, beauty and health. Now Softpllow Beauty Salon is not just a famous landmark of the sunny city of Almaty. During its existence, the complex has turned into a comfortable resort that combines the traditions of the East and high technologies.

To relax and get real pleasure, you do not need to spend time and money on a good country sanatorium. Everything that is required for a comfortable stay and the appearance of a good mood is located in the center of the southern capital of our Motherland.

A building we are proud of!

Our complex is located in a building that is considered a bright original architectural monument of republican significance and is under state protection. The complex was built in 1979-1982. and acquired the status of an exclusive attraction of the southern capital of Kazakhstan in the first years of its appearance. The grandiose and majestic building of Arasan Wellness & SPA instantly wins the hearts of our country's guests.

In 2016, restoration work was completed in Arasan Wellness & SPA and a major overhaul was carried out. We are proud that we managed to preserve the oriental flavor and uniqueness of our complex, improving its functional qualities - after all, the best architects in the world worked on the project.

Today, the bath and health complex Arasan Wellness & SPA is a combination of a magnificent interior and engineering thought of the XX and XXI centuries. The secret of the uniqueness of the complex lies in the combination of individual technologies and inimitable oriental architecture. The interior of Arasan Wellness & SPA is made of marble and other natural stones - materials known for their fantastic beauty, stunning energy and antibacterial properties.

We keep pace with the times, offering excellent conditions for recovery and rejuvenation.
